[Contributed Talk] Scientists Have an Inherent Prioritized Queue in Selecting Collaborations

July 1, 2024

title: “Scientists Have an Inherent Prioritized Queue in Selecting Collaborations” excerpt: The paper analyzes the temporal dynamics of co-authorship in scientific publications, finding a fat-tailed interval distribution between recurring collaborations, which can be explained by a proposed priority selection model simulating multi-agent team dynamics based on co-authorship willingness. The findings suggest non-Poisson activity patterns in scientific collaborations.

venue: ‘ICSSI’
